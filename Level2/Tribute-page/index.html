A P J Abdul Kalam

About the Legend

  • Abdul Kalam. Born on 15th October 1931 at Rameswaram in Tamil Nadu, Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, specialized in Aeronautical Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology.
  • Abdul Kalam sold newspapers to support his family as a child.
  • He failed to get into the Indian Air Force because he stood ninth on the list of candidates, and there were only eight vacancies.
  • He worked as a scientist at the Defence Research and Development Organisation(DRDO) and the India Space Research Organisation(ISRO) for manny years.
  • He is known as the "Missile Man of india" for his contributions to the development of India's missile program.
  • He was the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • He was a prolific writer and wrote over 20 books, inncluding his autobiography, Wings of Fire.
  • He was a popular speaker and was known for his inspirational talks to students and young people.
  • He died on July 27, 2015, while delivering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong
  • He was awarder the Bharat Ratna, India's highest cicilian award, in 1997.
